Process

I noticed that they had really great content, and noticed, as a football fan too, that Brazilians love football and love to share about their favorite clubs and players. So I suggested to Footstats owner to create a Social Network, the first social network designed for football fans. He was so excited that within a week we formed a new cross-functional team to make that project possible. It was amazing. I was able to create all Usability and interface for this social network, plus I also developed the front-end design for the desktop, responsive, and APP version. We used Angular and Ionic frameworks to make that possible. There are so many cool features.. it is really nice and it makes me very proud of it. After an year of dedication we launched Footstats with great success. Over a million users registered in the first week and today they have over 10 million users. After launching, ESPN Brasil hired Footstats owner to create a TV show with the weekly statistical highlights. I also suggested the development of a game, some kind of fantasy football, but this idea could not be taken because of budget issues.
